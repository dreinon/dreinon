## About Me

I build full-stack products, data systems and practical AI automations with TypeScript, Python, React, SQL and cloud/serverless tools.

I am a Data Science student at [Universitat Politècnica de València](https://www.upv.es/) and work as a freelance engineer through **Reinon Tech**, helping teams turn operational, data and product problems into maintainable software.

My background combines product engineering, analytics engineering and applied AI: I like understanding the real workflow first, then building tools that reduce manual work, improve traceability and support better decisions.

## What I Build

- 🧩 Full-stack web apps and SaaS workflows.
- ⚙️ Backends, REST APIs, serverless functions and third-party integrations.
- 📊 Data pipelines, SQL models, dashboards and reverse ETL workflows.
- 🛠️ Internal tools for operations, reporting and process automation.
- 🤖 Practical AI/LLM workflows focused on business efficiency.
